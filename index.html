Terrific Travel Tips

We want to help you traverse the world, seeing the culture, exploring the quirks and adventuring into the new or unknown!

There is something for everyone in Japan:

  • Big Foodie? Dine at the Michelin starred Tapas Molecular Bar in Tokyo with sky high views across the city!
  • Fans of the game Ghost of Tsushima? why not visit Iki and Tsushima Islands, where you can explore Monkey Rock and Kojima shrine or try some of the rarest Wagyu beef in the world!
  • Into your history? Visit Hiroshima peace memorial park and museum.
  • Want to experience what it is like to be a disney character? Be bowed at by thousands of Deer in the stunning city of Nara!
  • Interested in futuristic cityscapes? The incredible 3d billboards in Shibuya and Shinjuku are a must see
  • Want some instagram perfect photos? Go for a tour of Kyoto stopping at Fushimi Inari, the Golden pavillion and take the Bamboo forest walk at Arashiyama
  • Need a thrill? why not spend a couple of days at Universal Studios at Super Nintendo World, the wizarding world of Harry potter or through Jurassic park

Top things to do in Peru:

  • Take a helicopter ride over the Nazca Lines and see the famous "Astronaut" for yourselves!
  • Ride in a buggy over the sand dunes near Ica at Huacachina!
  • Travel in high class style on the Peru Rail Train from Puno to Cusco
  • Discover the floating homes and reed island communities on Lake Titicaca
  • Ponder the existence of an ancient advanced civilisation at Machu Picchu and Saqsaywaman
